Services

Project Inception

The planning of your project starts here. Working together, you will share your project needs, dreams and goals and I'll do my best.

More

Schematic Design

During this stage I will begin by preparing preliminary design sketches based on your requirements and budget to explore and illustrate possible options. I will review these options with you, pointing out the merits and responding to your input.

More

Design Development

This stage refines the agreed upon schematic design for a clearer definition of the scope and quality of the finished project. Further cost projections will determine how closely the design conforms to the budget expectations or what if any adjustments are required.

More

Construction Documents

When the design concepts have been decided, I prepare working drawings and specifications. These are required to obtain building permits and bids, and to guide the contractor in construction.

More

Bidding and Negotiation

I can advise you on your choice of contractor – usually done by calling for competitive bids. I will help evaluate the bids and select the contractor, and prepare the contract between you and the contractor.

More

Construction and Contract Administration

I will consult with you and advise you during construction. On-site visits will determine if construction is proceeding in general accordance with the contract, and that the design, materials, and workmanship meet with acceptable standards.

More

Substantial Performance of Construction

I provide regular progress reports to keep you informed, authorizes staged payments to the contractor and remains involved until the project is completed.
